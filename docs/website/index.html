Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for real people, real workflows, and real Macs. One tool. Zero friction.

☕
One-click activation
Left-click the menu bar icon to start your default duration instantly. No menus, no dialogs — just one click and your Mac stays awake.
1 click to activate
⏱
Flexible session lengths
Built-in presets from 15 minutes to 1 day, plus an indefinite mode. Add your own custom durations and pin your favourites as quick buttons. Your workflow, your timings.
🔋
Battery-aware
Set a threshold and KeepMirror stops automatically when charge drops below it.

🌙
Automation-ready
Auto-activates when Focus Mode or Screen Sharing starts.
🔔
Expiry alerts
Get a banner 5 min before your session ends, with an Extend +30m button built in.
💤
Power Nap support
Allow Power Nap lets Time Machine, iCloud Drive, and push email run in the background while your session is active — backed up, synced, and awake all at once.
How it works

Up and running in 30 seconds.

No account. No setup wizard. Just download and click.

1
⬇
Download and open

Grab the latest release, drag it to Applications, and open it. The ☕ icon appears in your menu bar — that's it. No installer wizard.

2
👆
Click to activate

Left-click to start your default session. Or press ⌥ click for the fastest trigger. Right-click for all options and settings.

3
📊
Watch the countdown

The menu header shows a live ring with remaining time. Optionally, a compact label like ☕ 42m sits right in your menu bar at a glance.

4
✅
Done — back to normal

When time's up, KeepMirror stops automatically. Your Mac returns to its normal sleep schedule. Zero cleanup required.
