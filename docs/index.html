Features

Built for real everyday camera checks

Everything you need for quick mirror access, snapshots, and mic confidence checks, without clutter.

Instant menu bar mirror

Open and close a live camera mirror directly from the menu bar icon.

One-tap snapshots

Tap the preview or press Space to capture. KeepMirror remembers your save location.

Countdown and formats

Use 0s, 3s, or 5s countdown and export in PNG, JPEG, or HEIF.

Optional mic meter

Enable mic check to show a reactive level badge and tune sensitivity.

Notch hover mode

On notch-capable Macs, reveal a compact floating mirror by hovering near the notch.

Global hotkey

Toggle mirror from anywhere with Command+Shift+M, or record your own shortcut.

Workflow

Ready in less than a minute

1

Install

Download KeepMirror from GitHub Releases and move it to Applications.

2

Grant camera access

Allow camera permission so the mirror preview can start.

3

Open mirror

Click the menu bar icon or use your global hotkey.

4

Capture

Tap preview or press Space. Configure format, countdown, and clipboard behavior in Settings.

Permissions

Minimal by design

KeepMirror asks only for what each feature needs.

Permission Why it is needed Without it
Camera Shows live mirror preview and enables photo capture. Mirror preview cannot run.
Microphone (optional) Powers live mic level meter when Mic Check is enabled. Mirror still works, but mic meter is unavailable.
Not required Accessibility, Input Monitoring, and Screen Recording are not used. No high-privilege input access needed.
